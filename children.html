TASK OF TODAY

Alicja Kwade is a polish artist. She works with sculptures and installations. She is based in Berlin. Alicja Kwade works with common materials like wood, glass and copper but manipulates the materiels through chemical processes. She also often includes sounds in her work.

Alicja Kwade worked with steel and rocks in the exhibition shown below. Try to find some rocks in your neighbourhood and organize them in a creative way, either alone or together with other materials.
